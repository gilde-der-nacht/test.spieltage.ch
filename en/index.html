Cancellation of the Luzerner Spieltage 2021

Dear friends,

It is with a heavy heart that as a result of the pandemic the Organising Committee has decided to cancel the Luzerner Spieltage 2021.

Smaller gaming events are being added to our calendar on a regular basis.

We hope to welcome you at the Luzerner Spieltagen 2022.
